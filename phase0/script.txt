The storm had finally passed, leaving the harbor town wrapped in a cold gray mist.
High above the cliffs, the old lighthouse stood silent for the first time in forty years.
Inside, the keeper's room remained untouched, as if time itself had forgotten it.
A small robot climbed the spiral stairs, its lantern casting long shadows on the stone.
It had come searching for the keeper's final message.
And on the desk, beneath a pile of weathered maps, it found the letter.
